WHAT IS VENT?

Sometimes, people are holding onto problems that are easier to share with strangers than with friends or family. Other times, people need a way to let off some steam.

Vent aims to solve both of these issues. It provides a safe space for two strangers to respectfully discuss their problems, as well as a space to just roast the shit out of each other. Best of all, it can be used on virtually any device. All you need is an internet connection!

HOW IT WORKS

1) Select what you want to do: VENT or ROAST.

2) Wait while we quickly match you up with another person...

3) And that's it. You're all set to Vent or Roast.
